Our Journey

Fenyal was born out of a passion for quality food and exceptional service. What started as a Premium Cafe in Malappuram in 2015 has now evolved into one of the most beloved dining establishments in Kerala.

Our founder, Chef Rahul, brought his culinary expertise from years of working in premium Cafes across India. His vision was simple: to create a place where traditional recipes meet contemporary flavors, all served in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Our Mission

To serve delicious, quality food that brings people together and creates memorable dining experiences.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and loved food service brand, known for exceptional quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
